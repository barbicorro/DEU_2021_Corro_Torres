Question 3

In case of a flood, which is the most important thing to rescue?

Correct!

Documentation

Although it’s not the first thing that comes to mind, it’s important to keep documents in a waterproof cotainer. Here are some things that should be safe: birth certificate, ID, passport, diplomas, important phone numbers (family/friends) since they will be inaccessible if your battery or phone dies.
